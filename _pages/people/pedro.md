## PEDRO KREUTZ WERLE
**PhD Candidate**

PEDRO KREUTZ WERLE received the B.Sc. and M.Eng. degrees in electronic and computer engineering from University College Dublin, Dublin, Ireland, in 2024 and 2025, respectively. During 2024–2025, he worked at the Intelligent Systems Lab at University College Dublin as a Graduate Researcher and joined as a Research Assistant. His research interests include embedded AI and energy-efficient architectures.
