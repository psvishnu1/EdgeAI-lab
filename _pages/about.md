Our research spans Edge AI, edge hardware, IoT and wearable sensing, and circuits, algorithms, and AI for biomedical devices. We design efficient, low-power systems that bring intelligence closer to sensors and the physical world - from custom silicon and embedded algorithms to full sensing pipelines for health and wellness applications.

We collaborate closely with universities across US and Europe, and welcome enquiries from prospective students and collaborators interested in edge computing, hardware-software co-design, and applied AI for biomedical sensing.
