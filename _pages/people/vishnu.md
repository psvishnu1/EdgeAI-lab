## Vishnu PS
**PhD Candidate**

Vishnu is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University College Dublin, Ireland, where he has been a Ph.D. student since May 2025 under the supervision of Dr. Deepu John and Prof. Bogdan Staszewski. His research focuses on low-power hardware accelerators for deep neural networks (DNNs). He received the B.Tech. degree in electronics and communication engineering from Government Model Engineering College (MEC), Thrikkakkara, India, in 2019, and the M.S. (by Research) degree in digital VLSI design from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Palakkad, India, in 2025. From 2019 to 2021, he worked as a Firmware Test Engineer (Bluetooth/Wi-Fi) with Broadcom Inc.
